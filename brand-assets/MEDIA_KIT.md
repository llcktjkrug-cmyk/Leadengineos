## Logo Usage

### Primary Logo (Wordmark)
- **File:** `logo-primary.png`
- **Use:** Main logo for most applications
- **Background:** White or light backgrounds

### Icon Logo (Monogram)
- **File:** `logo-icon.png`
- **Use:** Favicon, app icons, social media profiles
- **Minimum size:** 32x32px

### Dark Mode Logo
- **File:** `logo-dark.png`
- **Use:** Dark backgrounds
- **Background:** Navy (#0f172a) or darker

### Logo Clear Space
Maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the "L" in Lead.

### Logo Don'ts
- Do not stretch or distort the logo
- Do not change the logo colors
- Do not add effects (shadows, gradients, outlines)
- Do not place on busy backgrounds without sufficient contrast
- Do not rotate the logo

## Brand Voice

### Tone
- **Professional** but approachable
- **Confident** without being arrogant
- **Clear** and jargon-free
- **Helpful** and solution-oriented

### Key Messages
1. "Marketing fulfillment that runs itself"
2. "Stop hiring. Start automating."
3. "Professional content without the agency price tag"
4. "Built for operators, not marketers"

### Words We Use
- Operators (not "clients" or "customers")
- Fulfillment (not "creation" or "production")
- Automated (not "AI-generated")
- Professional (not "cheap" or "affordable")

### Words We Avoid
- "Cheap" or "budget"
- "AI-written" or "robot"
- "Easy" (implies low quality)
- "Unlimited" (we have clear limits)
