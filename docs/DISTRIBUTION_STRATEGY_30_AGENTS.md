# Lead Engine OS - 30-Agent Distribution Strategy

## Executive Summary

A sophisticated multi-channel distribution campaign deploying 30 specialized "agents" (channels/tactics) to drive awareness, leads, and conversions for Lead Engine OS launch. Target: Med spas, dental practices, and multi-location healthcare businesses.

---

## Distribution Architecture

### TIER 1: PAID ACQUISITION (Agents 1-8)
High-intent, immediate traffic channels with measurable ROI.

### TIER 2: ORGANIC & CONTENT (Agents 9-16)
Long-term authority building and SEO-driven acquisition.

### TIER 3: PARTNERSHIP & OUTREACH (Agents 17-22)
Relationship-based distribution through industry networks.

### TIER 4: COMMUNITY & SOCIAL (Agents 23-28)
Engagement-driven channels for brand awareness and trust.

### TIER 5: AUTOMATION & RETARGETING (Agents 29-30)
Conversion optimization and lead nurturing systems.

## RISK MITIGATION

1. **Budget Overrun:** Set hard caps per channel, review weekly
2. **Low Conversion:** A/B test continuously, pivot quickly
3. **Channel Saturation:** Diversify across all 30 agents
4. **Competitor Response:** Monitor and differentiate messaging
5. **Platform Changes:** Don't over-rely on any single channel
